Visual Aided Stories VAS

VAS is a youth-led enterprise of creatives, entrepreneurs and civic innovators who use art and storytelling as tools for social change.

Who We Are

Through art, VAS celebrates history and culture of Pan Africanism, raises awareness on important social issues and showcases local talent.

Social Currency

Through the arts program developed at VAS Hub, we have been able to identify talent and organizations in the creative industry such as creative directors and local street artists and provide them with the tools and resources needed to create and showcase murals in public and digital spaces.

Media Coverage

The Zingatia campaign remains relevant in 2021 and shows the importance of engaging with the masses (mass communication/behavioral change campaigns) in development areas and things that impact life and death. The campaign continues to receive coverage across print and digital media.

Our Programs

VAS runs regional programs and activities, which include Entrepreneurship Readiness program, Hosting art exhibitions, Art Series & Opportunities in cities like Dar and Mwanza.
